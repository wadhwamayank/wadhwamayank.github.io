Health

"It is health that is real wealth and not pieces of gold and silver." ~ Mahatma Gandhi

With educating the underprivileged, we also take care of the health aspect and continue to spread awareness regarding the same. We spread awareness among these children regarding important issues like hygiene, sanitation as well as sanitization. Better health is central to human happiness and well-being. It also makes an important contribution to economic progress, as healthy populations live longer, are more productive, and save more.

We believe that people as individuals can make this situation of global pandemic better by being aware, educated and by staying healthy. To keep the lives of people out of danger, our NGO: Antatah Gatva Foundation helps those who lack awareness and are deprived of basic amenities like health and education.

Behind every organization there is a team that works together like a family. If this very foundation of people is weak, it is impossible to proceed towards success. The founder of Antatah Gatva Foundation, Mr. Karan Magu believes that bringing together and building this foundation would have been impossible without the support and blessings of some people. He would like to give the credit of the success of this foundation to his grandparents, Mr. Ram Prakash Magu & Mrs. Shanti Devi. He would also like to thank his parents and brother.

There is a long list of people, who contributed in numerous ways to bring up this foundation and they are also the founders of this foundation. Their effort and good will is unmatched and cannot be expressed enough in the form of gratitude. These people are the strands of the rope that provide strength to this foundation and have helped in holding it together.

A Message

From The Founder

We all worked together as a team, as a family and tried to make a difference by helping those who needed us. I believe that I am just a medium and that the real credit for all the effort put into this foundation goes to each one of you, who contributed even a day or a week or a month in doing work for this very foundation. It is incomplete without all of you. So, thank you for your contribution. You are and will always remain a part of Antatah Gatva Foundation.

Donate to save a life.

Antatah Gatva Foundation works for the underprivileged who cannot afford education and cannot take care of their health. Our foundation helps these people in getting education and also helps them in gaining awareness about health care and hygiene. As it is a Non Governmental Organization, it generates funds from the people who wish to contribute for the cause. As we believe that mankind together can save itself from the burden of poverty and starvation, it is necessary that we help those who need our support both financially and morally. Hence, contribute to our noble cause as we can make a difference!
