Our Works

Antatah Gatva Foundation believes in spreading awareness about multiple stakeholders which help an individual in staying safe and healthy in these times of a global pandemic. We focus on educating individuals about how to stay out of risk and how to cope with the lockdown situation.
Following are the major concerns about which our NGO finds it necessary to spread awareness:

Controlling the Population Growth

Due to the global pandemic, COVID-19 multiple areas have been put under lockdown because of which most of the people are either unemployed or working from home. Due to excessive availability of free time and lack of work, most of the people are indulging into intercourse and most of the time it is unprotected, eventually resulting in unwanted pregnancies and child births. This is not only adding to the population of our country but also adding up to the financial burden of the families which is difficult to cope with due to the economic state of our country at the moment. So, it is necessary that we spread awareness among the population to prevent our country from becoming even more populated than before.

Taking Care of Health

The COVID-19 virus spreads due to physical contact, presence of an infected person in your surroundings and unhygienic conditions. Due to lack of sanitization, individuals become more vulnerable to the coronavirus and get infected easily. It is necessary that we wash hands and sanitize them and all the things we use along with the things which are brought into contact with other people as infected people act as the carriers of the virus. So, every individual should practice sanitization, maintain their hygiene and avoid human contact in order to stay healthy.

Reducing Poverty

Almost about 22% of people are below the poverty line in India and lack basic amenities like food, shelter, education. As education is an asset for an individual, it is important that we educate the less privileged so that they can make a living by getting a job. Educating them will also make them aware about the current situation and will help them in staying out of the risk of getting infected by the normal virus. Educating the underprivileged will not only empower them but also make it easy for them to afford basic amenities and hence would reduce the poverty in our country.

Effective Use of Resources

Since the global pandemic has indirectly affected the economic state of our country, it is absolutely necessary that we conserve the resources and restrict their use instead of spending them blindly. Resources like water and electricity should be conserved so that there are less power cuts and no water shortages. People should use the resources only to the extent where they are actually required and conserve them as much as possible in order to attain the financial stability of our country.

Employment Opportunities

More employment opportunities and better employment opportunities should be provided to those who are unemployed. The job opportunities should equal the qualification of the individual so that their abilities are put into maximum use and that they can earn the amount of money that they deserve. More employment can be introduced by encouraging more startup programs and finding angel investors who wish to sponsor the startups. This will increase employment and help people in staying focused on the right things.

Access to Technology

To spread more awareness and to spread education during the time of a global pandemic it is necessary that internet and technology like smartphones and/or laptops is available for those who dearly wish to learn. The Internet is essential for staying updated and aware about the world, it also increases the awareness of an individual and helps them empower themselves. In todayâ€™s world, every important information is accessed via the internet and hence it becomes even more necessary to increase its access to the areas where it is barely available.

Modern Approaches in Agriculture

Agriculture is the largest source of livelihood in India and majority of the rural households are dependent on agriculture for their livelihood. Most of them are small farmers who are land labours who often commit suicide due to the burden of debt. It is essential that waivers on farm loans are increased so that the income of farmers is increased and they do not die under the burden of debt. Modern approaches like removing interest rates on farm loans for small farmers should be executed so that more and more farmers get encouraged to take loans from banks and not from informal sector lenders.

Respecting Animals and Nature

As individuals it is our responsibility to take care of the welfare of animals and nature. We should respect animals and we should not ill treat them by throwing stones at them or harming them for fun. We should be insensitive towards these creatures and help them and save them at all costs. During the global pandemic most of the people have stopped feeding stray dogs and other stray animals. We should save them from starvation by feeding them food whenever possible. As nature balances out whatever we do, our behaviour towards nature and animals will definitely repay us one day.

Help the Underprivileged

We should help people who lack enough sources to survive. Most of the people are unemployed at the moment and are dying of starvation. It is necessary that we turn sensitive towards these people and save them at all costs for the sake of humanity.We can help them by buying them food and other essential things for their survival. We should focus on ways of employing these people so that they can earn a livelihood for themselves.

Awareness About the Situation

As individuals we should be aware about the conditions and situations in our surroundings and also around the globe. The more we stay aware, the more we stay out of the risk of getting involved in these situations. We as the responsible citizens of our country should actively participate in online forums and spread correct knowledge and information for the welfare of other people who are unaware about these situations.

Donate to save a life.

Antatah Gatva Foundation works for the underprivileged who cannot afford education and cannot take care of their health. Our foundation helps these people in getting education and also helps them in gaining awareness about health care and hygiene. As it is a Non Governmental Organization, it generates funds from the people who wish to contribute for the cause. As we believe that mankind together can save itself from the burden of poverty and starvation, it is necessary that we help those who need our support both financially and morally. Hence, contribute to our noble cause as we can make a difference!
