Education

"Education is the most powerful weapon we can use to change the world." ~ Nelson Mandel

Education is the biggest asset anybody can ever give to an individual. It holds more value than the rest of the riches. Even after being an essential requirement for the human resource, education still fails to reach many sections of our society. With the hope of imparting education and knowledge to every individual in our society, Antatah Gatva Foundation (a Non Governmental Organization) intends to educate those who are unaware about this asset and cannot afford to go to school. Our NGO is bearing all the cost and expenditure of underprivileged children and sending them to school in order to educate them. A total of 35 children as of now are being schooled and educated under our initiative.

Donate to save a life.

Antatah Gatva Foundation works for the underprivileged who cannot afford education and cannot take care of their health. Our foundation helps these people in getting education and also helps them in gaining awareness about health care and hygiene. As it is a Non Governmental Organization, it generates funds from the people who wish to contribute for the cause. As we believe that mankind together can save itself from the burden of poverty and starvation, it is necessary that we help those who need our support both financially and morally. Hence, contribute to our noble cause as we can make a difference!
